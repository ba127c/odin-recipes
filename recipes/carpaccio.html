Salmon Carpaccio

Description

Fresh salmon slices with refreshing lemon juice and cilantro with a kick of spicy onion slices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack sushi grade salmon
  • Little bit of cilantro
  • 1/2 onions
  • 1/2 lemon sliced in half
  • 1/2 teasppon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  1. Slice the fresh salmon into thin slices.
  2. Mix lemon juice, soy sauce, salt and pepper, and sliced onions together.
  3. Pour the sauce thoroughly on the platter of thinly sliced salmons.
  4. Place onions on the sides of salmons and place the washed cilantros on top of the plate.
  5. Enjoy!

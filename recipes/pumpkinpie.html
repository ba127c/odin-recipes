Pumpkin Pie

Description

A delicious pumpkin pie with a crispy crust and a smooth, creamy pumpkin filling.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. pumpkin puree
  • 14 oz condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 9 inch unbaked pie crust
  1. Preheat the oven, then whisk pumpkim puree, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt until smooth.
  2. Pour mixture into the pie crust and bake in the preheated oven.
  3. Reduce heat and continue to bake until a knife inserted 1 inch from the crust comes out clean and the center is jiggly.
  4. Cool down the pie and serve.
